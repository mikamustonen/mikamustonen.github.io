About me

Hi there! My name is Mika Mustonen. I currently work as a Data Scientist with the awesome and talented people at Futusome in Helsinki, Finland.

Before transitioning to data science, I used to be a theoretical/computational physicist and had the priviledge of working on nuclear models using some of the most powerful supercomputers in the world, most recently as an associate research scientist at Yale University. A list of my scholarly publications can be found in Google Scholar.
